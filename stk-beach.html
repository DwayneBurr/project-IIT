St kilda beach location

Easy to spot

St Kilda Beach, located 6 km south of Melbourne's city centre

700-metre sandy waterfront in Port Phillip Bay known for its stunning sunsets and skyline views.

Transport: Easily accessible from the city centre via a short tram ride

St Kilda beach landmark

st kilda pier

A popular spot for walking, fishing, and viewing the city skyline

A breakwater at the end of the pier is home to a colony of little penguins

The new pier has welcomed approximately 925,000 visitors since opening in December 2024

beach activities

dining & nightlife

The area is lined with numerous cafes, restaurants, and bars along the foreshore.

St kilda sea baths

A historic venue offering indoor heated seawater pools and spa facilities.

swimming and water sports

The beach is used for swimming, windsurfing, kiteboarding, and boating.
